SDG 7:

Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all

What is SDG 7?

Sustainable Development Goal 7 ensures access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all. It promotes renewable sources like solar, wind, and hydro energy.

Why Clean Energy Matters

Energy powers everything — schools, hospitals, and homes. Clean energy reduces pollution and protects the planet.

Action in Practice

Renewable Sources

Use solar panels and other green technologies.

Energy Efficiency

Reduce waste by using energy wisely.

Innovation and Access

Support programs bringing power to rural and low-income areas.

What We Can Do

At our school and homes, we can help save and protect water.

  • Turn off unused lights and devices
  • Learn about renewable energy
  • Support energy-saving initiatives
  • Promote clean energy awareness

“Clean energy lights the way to a sustainable tomorrow.”
