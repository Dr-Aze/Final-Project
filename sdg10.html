SDG 10:

Reduce inequality within and among countries.

What is SDG 10?

This goal aims to ensure everyone has equal chances in life — regardless of age, gender, disability, race, or background.

Why It Matters

Inequality limits growth and divides communities. Reducing inequality builds fairness and unity.

Action in Practice

Decent Jobs

Creating employment opportunities that are fair and safe is crucial for development.

Fair Wages

Ensuring workers earn enough to live with dignity strengthens communities.

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Supporting small businesses and innovation drives sustainable growth.

What We Can Do

  • Speak out against discrimination
  • Support inclusive policies and programs
  • Promote fairness and empathy

“Equality is not a privilege, it is a right that strengthens every society.””
