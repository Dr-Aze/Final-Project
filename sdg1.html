SDG 1:

End poverty in all its forms everywhere

What is SDG 1?

Sustainable Development Goal 1 aims to end poverty in all its forms everywhere. It focuses on providing basic needs such as food, shelter, and social protection, so that every person can live a life free from deprivation.

Why Ending Poverty Matters

Poverty affects education, health, and opportunities for families and communities. By reducing poverty, societies become stronger, more resilient, and more equitable.

Action in Practice

Access to Basic Needs

Ensuring everyone has food, clean water, and shelter is a foundation for a better future.

Social Protection Programs

Providing support for vulnerable communities helps them recover from crises and improve their lives.

Economic Opportunities

Creating fair jobs and empowering communities helps lift people out of poverty sustainably.

What We Can Do

At our school, we can raise awareness, support community projects, and help those in need.

  • Share resources with those less fortunate
  • Support fundraising and community initiatives
  • Promote fairness and empathy
  • Encourage policies that help reduce poverty
