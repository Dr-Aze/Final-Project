SDG 11:

Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

What is SDG 11?

SDG 11 focuses on creating urban spaces that are safe, accessible, and environmentally friendly. It emphasizes affordable housing, green spaces, public transport, and disaster-resilient communities.

Why sustainability Matters

Sustainable cities improve quality of life, reduce pollution, and make communities more resilient to disasters. Proper planning ensures everyone can thrive in urban environments.

Action in Practice

Affordable Housing

Ensure housing is safe and affordable for all.

Green Spaces

Promote parks and clean areas for recreation and health.

Efficient Transport

Develop public transportation systems that reduce pollution and traffic.

What We Can Do

  • Participate in local clean-ups
  • Support public transport initiatives
  • Advocate for safe and sustainable community planning

“Sustainable cities grow not just in size, but in harmony, safety, and inclusivity.”
