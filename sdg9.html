SDG 9:

Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation

What is SDG 9?

SDG 9 focuses on developing reliable infrastructure, fostering innovation, and supporting sustainable industrial growth.

Why Innovation Matters

Strong infrastructure and innovation drive economic growth, connect communities, and solve environmental and social challenges.

Action in Practice

Sustainable Infrastructure

Develop roads, energy systems, and digital networks responsibly.

Innovation & Research

Encourage technology and creativity that solve local and global problems.

Inclusive Industry

Support industries that create jobs and protect the environment.

What We Can Do

Support local innovation projects, recycle materials for creative use, and promote science and technology education.

  • Explore STEM careers
  • Promote sustainable solutions and technologies

“Innovation and resilient infrastructure are the pathways to a sustainable future.”
