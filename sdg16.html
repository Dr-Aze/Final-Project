SDG 16:

Promote peaceful and inclusive societies, provide access to justice, and build effective institutions.

What is SDG 16?

SDG 16 focuses on fostering justice, reducing violence, and ensuring accountability in governance. It encourages strong institutions that protect human rights and promote peace.

Why It Matters

Peaceful and fair societies allow people to live safely and contribute to development. Corruption, injustice, and conflict prevent communities from thriving.

Action in Practice

Justice & Rights

Ensure equal access to legal systems and human rights protection.

Peace & Security

Promote conflict resolution and social cohesion.

Strong Institutions

Support transparent and accountable governance.

What We Can Do

  • Practice fairness, honesty, and respect
  • Advocate for justice and equality
  • Participate in community-building initiatives

“Peace and justice are the pillars upon which true progress stands.”
