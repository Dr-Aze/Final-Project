SDG 15:

Protect, restore, and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems.

What is SDG 15?

SDG 15 aims to conserve forests, combat desertification, and prevent biodiversity loss. It promotes sustainable land management and ecosystem restoration.

Why It Matters

Healthy ecosystems provide food, clean air, and water. Loss of biodiversity threatens life on Earth and human well-being.

Action in Practice

Forest Protection

Prevent deforestation and promote reforestation programs.

Biodiversity Conservation

Protect endangered species and habitats.

Sustainable Land Use

Manage farming, mining, and urban growth responsibly.

What We Can Do

  • Plant trees and maintain green spaces
  • Support wildlife conservation
  • Avoid products that harm ecosystems

“The Earth is our shared home, nurture it, and it will sustain generations to come.”
