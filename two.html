SDG 2:

End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition and promote sustainable agriculture

Together, we can build a world where everyone has enough nutritious food to thrive.

What is SDG 2?

Sustainable Development Goal 2 seeks to end hunger, achieve food security, and promote sustainable agriculture. It aims to ensure that everyone — especially children — can access healthy and sufficient food.

Why Zero Hunger Matters

Hunger affects growth, health, and learning. By achieving zero hunger, communities become healthier, more productive, and more resilient.

Action in Practice

Nutritious Food Access

Providing balanced meals helps children grow and communities flourish.

Sustainable Farming

Supporting farmers and sustainable agriculture ensures food for generations to come.

Ending Malnutrition

Addressing malnutrition improves health and unlocks potential for all.

What We Can Do

At our school, we can help reduce hunger and promote awareness about food security.

  • Donate food or organize drives
  • Support local farmers and sustainable food programs
  • Encourage healthy eating habits
  • Educate peers about hunger and nutrition
