SDG 6:

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all

What is SDG 6?

Sustainable Development Goal 6 aims to guarantee the availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all. It focuses on clean drinking water, good hygiene, and proper waste treatment systems.

Why Clean Water Matters

Clean water is essential for health, education, and livelihoods. Without it, diseases spread and opportunities are lost. Ensuring sanitation and hygiene protects communities and the environment.

Action in Practice

Safe Drinking Water

Provide access to clean, affordable water sources for everyone.

Proper Sanitation

Encourage safe waste management and hygiene practices.

Water Conservation

Protect rivers, lakes, and groundwater through responsible use.

What We Can Do

At our school and homes, we can help save and protect water.

  • Turn off taps when not in use
  • Keep water sources clean
  • Support sanitation programs
  • Raise awareness about water conservation

“Water is life — protecting it means protecting our future.”
