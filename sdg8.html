SDG 8:

Promote sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment and decent work for all

What is SDG 8?

Sustainable Development Goal 8 promotes inclusive and sustainable economic growth, employment, and decent work for everyone.

Why Decent Work Matters

Fair jobs reduce poverty and inequality. Economic growth drives innovation, opportunity, and well-being.

Action in Practice

Fair Employment

Ensure safe working conditions and equal pay.

Innovation and Skills

Encourage entrepreneurship and lifelong learning.

Sustainable Growth

Support responsible production and fair business.

What We Can Do

At our school and homes, we can help save and protect water.

  • Learn practical and job-ready skills
  • Support fair trade and local businesses
  • Value hard work and equality
  • Encourage responsible economic practices

“Decent work means dignity, opportunity, and progress for all.”
