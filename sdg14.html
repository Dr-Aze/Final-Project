SDG 14:

Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources.

What is SDG 14?

SDG 14 focuses on protecting marine ecosystems, regulating fishing, and reducing ocean pollution. Healthy oceans are essential for food, biodiversity, and climate regulation.

Why Matters

Overfishing, pollution, and climate change threaten marine life. Protecting oceans ensures livelihoods and food security for millions.

Action in Practice

Marine Conservation

Create protected areas and restore habitats.

Reduce Pollution

Control plastic, chemical, and oil waste in waterways.

Sustainable Fisheries

Support responsible fishing practices that preserve ecosystems.

What We Can Do

  • Reduce plastic use and dispose of waste properly
  • Participate in coastal clean-ups
  • Support sustainable seafood choices

“The ocean connects us all, protecting it means protecting life itself.”
