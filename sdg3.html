SDG 3:

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages

What is SDG 3?

Sustainable Development Goal 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages. It focuses on reducing preventable diseases, improving healthcare access, and promoting mental and physical well-being.

Why Health Matters

Good health is the foundation for learning, working, and living fully. By investing in health, communities grow stronger and more resilient.

Action in Practice

Access to Healthcare

Ensuring everyone can see a doctor, get vaccinations, and receive treatments saves lives.

Healthy Lifestyles

Encouraging exercise, nutritious diets, and mental wellness improves quality of life.

Preventive Measures

Education on hygiene, disease prevention, and safety protects communities.

What We Can Do

At our school, we can promote healthy habits and support wellness programs.

  • Wash hands regularly and practice hygiene
  • Encourage balanced diets and exercise
  • Support mental health awareness
  • Participate in health campaigns and initiatives
