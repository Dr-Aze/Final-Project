SDG 12:

Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.

What is SDG 11?

SDG 12 promotes the efficient use of resources, reducing waste, and encouraging sustainable production. It aims to minimize environmental impact while meeting society’s needs.

Why It Matters

Overconsumption depletes resources and harms ecosystems. Sustainable choices protect the planet for future generations.

Action in Practice

Reduce Waste

Encourage recycling, composting, and responsible disposal.

Sustainable Production

Support businesses that use eco-friendly methods.

Resource Efficiency

Promote energy and water conservation in homes and industries.

What We Can Do

  • Reuse, reduce, and recycle materials
  • Buy sustainable and eco-friendly products
  • Save energy and water

“Our choices today shape the planet of tomorrow, consume wisely, live responsibly.”
